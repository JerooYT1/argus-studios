Better Ores

January 20, 2023 | Jeroo

Diamond Netherite

It is when wearing it will give you the effect of strength and resistance, with a damage of 10>5 in the tools and a durability of 1000.

Iron Netherite

Wearing this armor will give you the Haste effect, which will make you chop the blocks faster, the damage of these tools is 9>4 and a durability of 750.

Golden Netherite

This armor will not give you effects, but it will give you immunity to piglin attacks, basically the same as gold armor but with more durability and protection, with 8>3 damage and a durability of 650.

Emerald Netherite

This armor when worn will give you the village hero effect, which will make villagers sell you things for less, along with tool damage of 10>5 and durability of 850
